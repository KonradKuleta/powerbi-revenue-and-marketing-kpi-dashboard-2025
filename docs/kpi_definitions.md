# KPI Definitions

**Scope note:** Efficiency KPIs (ROAS, CPA, CAC, AOV) are calculated for **paid channels only**
(Organic excluded). Overall revenue visuals include **all channels** unless stated otherwise.

## Net Revenue
Sum of order net revenue (after discounts/returns as modeled in the synthetic dataset).
- Measure: `Net Revenue`

## Paid Revenue (Non-Organic)
Net Revenue excluding Organic.
- Measure: `Paid Revenue`

## Marketing Spend (Paid Spend)
Daily marketing spend for paid channels (Organic excluded).
- Measure: `Paid Spend`

## ROAS (Paid)
Paid Revenue / Paid Spend.
- Measure: `Paid ROAS`

## CPA (Paid)
Paid Spend / Non-Organic Orders.
- Measure: `CPA`

## CAC (Paid)
Paid Spend / New Customers.
- Measure: `CAC`

## AOV (Paid)
Paid Revenue / Non-Organic Orders.
- Measure: `AOV`

## Revenue Target
Monthly revenue target mapped to the date dimension.
- Measure: `Revenue Target`

## Revenue % of Target
Net Revenue / Revenue Target.
- Measure: `Revenue % of Target`
